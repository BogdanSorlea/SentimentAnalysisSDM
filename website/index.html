User review analysis

This the page presenting our Social Data Modelling project. Our goal was to analyse the reviews of movies and books left by the users on various websites. In the first part we look at the connection between sentiment and ratings. Other aspects of the sites are seen as well. Using that knowledge we go to the second part where we hope to extend the LabMT dataset. After that attempt we return to more familiar methods of Machine Learning in part three. There we take a closer look at the content of the review. Click on the links below to see more.
